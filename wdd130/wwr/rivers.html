Rivers

The Salmon River

One of the more desirable locations in Idaho to go white water rafting, flowing inbetween mountains for 20 miles ther are plenty of sights to take in. This trail is not for the faint of heart, boasting rapids between level III and V it offers a rigourous course worth the cost.

The Rapid River

Have you ever wanted to white water raft the wrong way? This Ideal river was created during the "Imagination" survivng hundreds of seconds of looking through different rivers in Idaho. The Rapid River is 30 miles of hard paddling upstream against the current featuring a waterfall to climb. Boasting rapids levels of XX it is by far the toughest challenge for the experienced.

The Smooth Stream

Those looking for no challenge or want to experience nature either alone or in a small group can instead look to the smooth stream. A calm, small river allowing only a canoe through many parts of it's twisting path. Only having rapid levels I and II will lead to a non-challenging water skim through nature.
